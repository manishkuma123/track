Free English Courses to Improve Communication (with Links)

1️⃣ Career English – Coursera
🔗 https://www.coursera.org/learn/careerdevelopment

🕒 ~12–15 hours | 🎓 Free with certificate

2️⃣ Speak Fluently – Coursera
🔗 https://www.coursera.org/learn/speak-english-professionally

🕒 ~10–12 hours | 🎓 Free with certificate

3️⃣ BBC Learning English – BBC English
🔗 https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish

🕒 Ongoing (15–20 mins/day) | 🗣️ Listening, grammar, vocabulary

4️⃣ Perfect English Grammar – Grammar Lessons
🔗 https://www.perfect-english-grammar.com/free-grammar-lessons.html

🕒 ~5–8 hours total | ✍️ Grammar with examples & practice

5️⃣ Elllo.org – Real Listening Practice
🔗 https://elllo.org/

🕒 Ongoing (10–15 mins/day) | 🎧 Audio lessons + quizzes

6️⃣ EnglishClass101 – YouTube Learning
🔗 https://www.youtube.com/user/ENGLISHCLASS101

🕒 Ongoing | 📺 Video-based learning (Beginner to Advanced)

7️⃣ Communication for Beginners – Free Udemy Course
🔗 https://www.udemy.com/course/communication-skills-for-beginners/

🕒 ~1.5–2 hours | 🗨️ Basic communication skills

8️⃣ Writing Skills – edX (UC Berkeley)
🔗 https://www.edx.org/course/academic-and-business-writing

🕒 ~8–10 hours | ✍️ Business, academic writing | 🎓 Free (audit mode)
